Welcome Dreamer and Allies

Note that this is the beta website for the offical Dreamers Club of Marco Antonio Firebaugh for the time being.

The Deramers Club is an educational club empowering our youth, their families, and our commuity to know their educational rights and continue their struggle in pursuit for higher education. The club aims to raise awareness about the issues that impact our community by providing workshops, speakers and activiteis that will educatioe the public on said issues such as the California Dream Act, AB540, DACA, immigration legislation, immigration status, driver license, financial, legal and health resources. By joining our club we hope you will learn about the challenges and resiliency that characterize our undocument students and community.
